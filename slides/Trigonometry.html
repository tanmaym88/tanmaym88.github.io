Introduction to Trigonometry

  • A branch of Mathematics that studies the relationships between the ratios of side lengths and angles of a triangle. It also involves solving problems related to the ratios.
  • It gets it names from the Greek words: “treîs” (three) + “gōnía” (angles) + “metron” (measure).
  • It has its pplications in: Civil Engineering, Astronomy, Navigation, Acoustics, Optics, Architecture, etc.
  • Has been studied by mankind for thousands of years.
